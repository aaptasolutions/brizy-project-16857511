ENAMELLED RECTANGULAR WINDING WIRES

Circuit Electrical Trading (CET) offers Enamelled Rectangular Copper Wires designed explicitly for low and medium-voltage electrical machines, including motors, generators, and transformers. These wires have a cross-section ranging from 3.0 to 75.0 square millimetres, ensuring compatibility with various applications. With insulation Class options ranging from 105 to 220 degrees Celsius, our Enamelled Rectangular Copper Wires provide reliable performance and protection under different operating conditions. Trust CET to provide high-quality wires that meet industry standards and exceed expectations, empowering your electrical systems with efficiency and durability.
